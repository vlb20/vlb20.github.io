I am a `PhD student` in the [SEART](https://seart.si.usi.ch) research group at the [Software Institute](https://si.usi.ch), [USI Lugano](https://www.usi.ch/en), supervised by [Prof. Gabriele Bavota](https://www.inf.usi.ch/faculty/bavota/) and co-supervised by [Dr. Rosalia Tufano](https://www.inf.usi.ch/phd/tufanr/). My research focuses on `AI for Software Engineering`.

Previously, I completed my Master's degree in `Computer Engineering` at the University of Naples Federico II with 110/110 with honors, where I worked on ontology-driven hazard generation for autonomous driving systems under the supervision of [Prof. Roberto Pietrantuono](http://wpage.unina.it/roberto.pietrantuono/index.html).
